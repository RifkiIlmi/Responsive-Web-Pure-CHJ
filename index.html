About

80% of the world population has a Quirk. There are infinite types of Quirks, and it is extremely unlikely to find two people who have the exact same power, unless they are closely related. These particular abilities have allowed the development of a new category of people: Heroes

U.A. High School is an academy where students learn and train to become heroes. It is one of the main settings in which the roleplay will take place in.

U.A. is the #1 ranked high school for heroics and is considered as the top Hero Academy in the world. Students are separated into specific departments and classes, allowing students to decide what is the best way they can utilize themselves for their probable entry into the pro-hero world.
